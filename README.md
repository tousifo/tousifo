## 👨‍💻 About Me

I am **K.M. Tousif Bin Parves** — a **Lecturer in Computer Science & Engineering**, **Machine Learning engineer**, and **robotics builder**.

I build practical AI and robotics systems across:

- 🤖 Robotics & Embedded Systems  
- 🧠 Machine Learning, Deep Learning & Computer Vision  
- ⚛️ Quantum Machine Learning  
- 📚 AI-powered education systems  
- 🛠️ MLOps, rapid prototyping, and real-world deployment  

My focus is simple: **turn technical ideas into working systems** — from humanoid robots and university bus tracking to intelligent academic assistants and applied ML solutions.
